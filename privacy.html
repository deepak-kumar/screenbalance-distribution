Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Zero Data Collection

ScreenBalance is built on the philosophy of privacy-first focus. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, screen time statistics, or unlock events to any external servers. Everything stays on your device.

2. On-Device Processing

All focus metrics, presence scores, and history logs are processed and stored locally using Apple's secure on-device Storage. We use the DeviceActivity and FamilyControls frameworks to monitor focus sessions, and this data never leaves your iPhone.

3. No Third-Party Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Your behavior in the app is yours alone.

4. Data Retention

History logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days on your device (for Pro users) or 7 days (for free users). You can clear all data at any time by deleting the app.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please reach out via our Support Page.
